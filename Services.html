Talbert’s Automotive Tire is a family-run business in Hillsborough, NC. Since 1970, we’ve been offering motorists of the local and nearby areas, unrivaled auto services. We’ve been a staple in the community ever since the doors to Talbert’s opened. Compared to most big chain auto shops, our privately-owned shop provides the most personalized services to our valued customers. We are also ASE Certified by the National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence.

Visit Talbert’s Automotive Tire today for auto service you won’t find at any other shop in the area. There’s a reason the doors to our shop have been open all these years. We hope you become a member of our Talbert’s family
